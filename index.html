What are Salmon Cookies?

Pat has developed a recipe for a coffee-time confection called Salmon Cookies.

They're cookies made into the shape of a salmon, and are actually made with salmon

They are the ideal match for a steaming cup of coffee on a gray Seattle morning. Or gray Seattle afternoon.
